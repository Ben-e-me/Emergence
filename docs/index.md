# Emergence

Emergence is a browser-based, canvas-driven experience that uses Conway&apos;s Game of Life to communicate the principle of emergence.

The project is designed as both:

- A **guided narrative** that introduces emergence as a lived, visual phenomenon.
- An **exploration tool** that lets people play with rules, seeds, and patterns.
